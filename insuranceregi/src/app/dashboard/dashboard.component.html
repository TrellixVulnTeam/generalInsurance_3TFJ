Motor Insurance

Motor insurance is a mandatory insurance cover that every vehicle owner in India must-have. Be it a car, truck, tractor or two-wheeler, every vehicle should be secured under an insurance policy of its own. Its main objective is to give complete protection against physical damage or loss sustained by the insured vehicle from natural and man-made calamities along with any third-party liabilities arising out of the insured vehicle.
